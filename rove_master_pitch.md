*   Menstruation: The body is shedding the uterine lining, losing blood, iron, zinc, and inflammatory prostaglandins are high.
*   Follicular: Oestrogen begins its climb. The body is in a building and regeneration phase. Energy, metabolic rate, and insulin sensitivity are at their best.

**The Clinical Goal:** Replace what is lost, reduce inflammation, prime the hormonal axis for optimal oestrogen metabolism, and support the rise of follicle-stimulating hormone (FSH).

**Formulation:**

| Ingredient | Dose | Clinical Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| **Ferrous Bisglycinate (Iron)** | 25 mg | Highly bioavailable, non-constipating form critical for addressing India's endemic anaemia. Ferrous bisglycinate has 2.4x better absorption than ferrous sulphate. |
| **Vitamin C (Ascorbic Acid)** | 80 mg | Co-administered with iron to enhance non-haem iron absorption by up to 67%. Also a cofactor for collagen synthesis and immune function. |
| **Vitamin B12 (Methylcobalamin)** | 500 mcg | The active, methylated form most bioavailable for South Asian vegetarian populations with endemic B12 deficiency. Supports red blood cell formation and neurological function. |
| **Folate (Methylfolate 5-MTHF)** | 400 mcg | The bioavailable form of folate (over folic acid), essential for cell regeneration and oestrogen metabolism. The MTHF form bypasses the MTHFR gene mutation prevalent in South Asian populations. |
| **Zinc (Zinc Picolinate)** | 15 mg | Significantly depleted during menstruation. Essential for FSH signalling, immune regulation, and reduction of prostaglandin-driven cramping. Clinical studies show zinc supplementation reduces dysmenorrhoea severity. |

| **DIM (Diindolylmethane)** | 100 mg | A crucifer-derived compound that supports healthy oestrogen metabolism — specifically promoting the conversion of oestrogen to its safer 2-hydroxy metabolite. Directly relevant to oestrogen-dominant conditions common in PCOS. |

## PART IV: THE ROVE APP — THE HORMONAL LIFESTYLE ENGINE

### Architecture Overview

The Rove App is not a tracker. It is a **Hormonal Lifestyle Operating System**. Its primary output is not data — it is a personalised daily plan. Every morning, the user opens Rove and receives three things: what to eat, how to move, and what to take. This is the core value proposition that no competitor delivers.

---

### Core Feature Set

**1. Cycle Intelligence Engine (The Foundation)**
*   Users input their last period date and average cycle length during onboarding.
*   The algorithm calculates current phase (Menstrual, Follicular, Ovulatory, Luteal) and current cycle day.
*   This data point becomes the master variable that drives every downstream recommendation in the app.
*   Unlike Flo and Clue, cycle data in Rove is not presented as an output — it is consumed as an input to generate personalised action plans.

**2. The AI Diet Coach (The Differentiator)**
*   Powered by a Large Language Model fine-tuned with strict South Asian dietary constraints:
    *   Protein Enforcement: Every meal must hit a minimum of 15-20g of protein. For vegetarian profiles, native sources (sattu, paneer, dal combinations, whey) are mandated.
    *   Glycemic Control: All carbohydrate suggestions must be paired with fiber or fat. Native complex carbs (jowar, bajra, ragi) are prioritised over refined wheat and rice.
    *   Iron Protocol Override: During the Menstrual phase, the AI automatically injects highly bioavailable Indian iron sources (jaggery, spinach, amaranth, dates) paired with Vitamin C into every meal plan.
*   The AI generates fresh, culturally authentic, ready-to-cook recipes daily — not a static library of 200 articles.

**3. Phase-Synced Movement Plans**
*   Follicular/Ovulatory Phases: High-intensity interval training, resistance training, and strength work are prioritised. Oestrogen enhances muscle protein synthesis and pain tolerance. This is the time to push.
*   Luteal Phase: The workout engine shifts to cortisol-lowering protocols — somatic flows, yoga, and restorative movement. Maintains active habits without triggering the cortisol-insulin spike that worsens Luteal symptoms.
*   Menstrual Phase: Gentle movement, walking, and breathwork. Reduces prostaglandin-driven inflammation without exacerbating hormonal fatigue.

**4. The Insights Engine**
*   Real-time, phase-specific health insights tailored to the user's current hormonal state.
*   Proactive, predictive alerts: *"You are 3 days from your Luteal phase. Begin reducing caffeine today to pre-empt the anxiety spike."*
*   Habit tracking with phase-aware contextualisation — a bad sleep score in the Luteal phase is flagged differently to the same score in the Follicular phase.

**5. The "Learn" Library (South Asian Specific)**
*   A curated, science-backed editorial library covering:
    *   The Indian Vegetarian's Guide to Cycle-Synced Protein
    *   Why 'Eat Less, Move More' Fails the South Asian Metabolism
    *   Ayurveda Meets Modern Hormonal Science
    *   The PCOS Epidemic in India: How to Eat for Your Hormones
    *   Managing Visceral Fat with Phase-Synced Resistance Training

**6. PCOS & Condition-Specific Modes**
*   During onboarding, users can flag PCOS, Thyroid conditions, or Insulin Resistance.
*   The algorithm dynamically adjusts: dairy is reduced and replaced with plant-based alternatives; anti-androgenic botanicals (spearmint, methi) are foregrounded in meal plans; supplement timing notifications align with managing the PCOS hormonal axis.
